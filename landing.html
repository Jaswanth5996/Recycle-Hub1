Despite the pervasive environmental impact of plastic pollution, there exists a significant lack of awareness among the general populace regarding the detrimental effects of improper plastic disposal. As a result, plastic waste is indiscriminately discarded, leading to environmental degradation and posing severe threats to ecosystems and public health. In response to this pressing issue, we aim to implement a solution that incentivizes plastic recycling by establishing a system for collecting plastic waste directly from individuals. By offering fair compensation for collected plastic, we seek to encourage community participation and promote responsible waste management practices.
