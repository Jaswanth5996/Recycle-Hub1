THE PLASTIC PROBLEM

1.8 billion tonnes of GHG emissions generated by plastics lifecycle.

Every year more than 390 million tonnes of plastic are produced globally.

More than 22 million tonnes of plastic waste leak into the environment and oceans yearly.

Globally, only 9% of plastic waste is recycled, 50% is landfilled, 22% is mismanaged, and 19% incinerated.

About Us
The mission of our Team is to tackle the global plastic pollution crisis head-on by implementing innovative recycling solutions that minimize environmental harm and promote circular economy principles.

Through our collective efforts, we strive to reduce the impact of plastic waste while maximizing resource recovery and fostering sustainable practices.
